# paperclip

Use the Paperclip control-plane workflow:

1. read assigned issues and dependencies
2. execute scoped work
3. leave durable updates in issue comments/documents
4. hand off explicitly with owner and next action

Do not bypass company governance, approval gates, or budget policy.
